Why I'm Running

I'm running for Cooper City Commission because I believe in the importance of service, and I feel most useful when I am serving others and giving back. My wife and I live in Cooper City because it is the best possible environment to raise a family. We love Cooper City, and I want to help keep it special — to ensure it remains safe, affordable, and livable for both new families putting down roots and longtime residents who have built this community over decades.

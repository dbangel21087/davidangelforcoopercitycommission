About David

A Cooper City Family Man & Community Leader

I'm David Angel — attorney, father of two, and proud Cooper City resident. My wife and I chose this city to raise our family because of its exceptional schools, safe neighborhoods, and strong sense of community. That's exactly what I want to protect.

Cooper City is a special place, and I believe it deserves leadership that truly understands what makes it great. I'm running for District 3 Commissioner because I'm ready to roll up my sleeves and work for the families who call this city home.

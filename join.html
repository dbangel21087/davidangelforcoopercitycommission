How You Can Help

There's a Place for Everyone on This Team

Grassroots campaigns succeed when the community comes together. You don't need to be a political insider or have a lot of free time — even small contributions of time or treasure make a real difference in a local race like this one.

District 3 is decided by neighbors talking to neighbors. When you volunteer, you're not just helping a campaign — you're helping shape the future of the city you live in.

  • Canvass & Knock Doors Talk to your neighbors and share David's message. We'll provide all the materials you need.
  • Phone Bank Call likely voters from home or at one of our campaign offices. Flexible scheduling available.
  • Yard Signs Show the neighborhood where you stand. Request a yard sign and we'll get it to you.
  • Spread the Word Online Follow and share our social media posts. Word of mouth is powerful — even digitally.
  • Host a House Party Invite friends and neighbors to meet David in your home or backyard. Contact us to schedule.

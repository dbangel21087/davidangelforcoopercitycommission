My Priorities

01
Priority One

Public Safety

Public safety is the foundation for everything else we want to build in Cooper City. I support fully funding our police and fire services, investing in proactive safety measures, and maintaining the high standards that give families peace of mind.

02
Priority Two

Infrastructure

Strong infrastructure keeps a city running smoothly and protects our long-term physical and financial health. I will support investment in infrastructure improvements and proactive maintenance to address issues early and protect taxpayers from the high costs of deferred maintenance.

03
Priority Three

Quality of Life

Cooper City's greatest strength is its sense of community. I am committed to preserving the character that makes this a special place to raise a family — safe neighborhoods, strong schools, well-maintained parks, and community events that bring us together — while thoughtfully expanding amenities that serve residents of all ages.

About Me

Father. Attorney. Cooper City Neighbor.

My Story

Rooted in Cooper City, Ready to Serve

I'm David Angel — an attorney, a husband, and a dad to two wonderful kids who go to school right here in Cooper City. My wife and I didn't end up here by accident. We chose Cooper City because of its incredible schools, its safe streets, its beautiful parks, and a community that still feels like a neighborhood.

We want our kids to grow up here. We want them to have the same opportunities we've had — to play in well-maintained parks, to attend top-rated schools, and to live in a community where neighbors look out for each other. That's why I'm running.

As an attorney, I've spent my career solving complex problems, advocating for people who need a voice, and navigating systems on behalf of others. Those skills translate directly to public service — and I'm ready to put them to work for District 3 and for all of Cooper City.

Cooper City is special. It's one of the best-kept secrets in South Florida — a safe, family-friendly, well-run city that punches above its weight. But staying special takes intentional leadership. It means saying no to overdevelopment that threatens our character, yes to investments in public safety and infrastructure, and always keeping families at the center of every decision.

I'm not a career politician. I'm your neighbor. And I'm asking for the honor of serving our community on the Cooper City Commission.

My Priorities Get Involved
Our Family

Invested in Cooper City's Future

My family and I are proud to call Cooper City home. As a parent, I experience first-hand what works in our community and what needs our attention. From the playgrounds our kids enjoy to the roads we drive every day, I care about the details that make daily life better.

When I'm not practicing law or attending city meetings, you'll find me at the park with my boys, cheering at youth sports games, or volunteering in the community. Cooper City isn't just where I live — it's where my heart is.

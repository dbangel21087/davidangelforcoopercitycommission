Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

David Angel for Cooper City Commission ("we," "our," or "the Campaign") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website and supporters of our campaign. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including your name, email address, phone number, home address, and zip code when you sign up through our website forms. We also collect standard web analytics data such as page views and browser type through third-party services.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used to send you campaign updates, event invitations, and volunteer opportunities. We may also use your contact information to follow up regarding your expressed interest in the campaign. We will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties outside the campaign except as required by law or Florida campaign finance regulations.

Campaign Finance Disclosures

In accordance with Florida law, contributions to political campaigns are a matter of public record. Donor information including name, address, occupation, and contribution amount may be reported to the Florida Division of Elections as required. This information becomes publicly available. Contributions are not tax-deductible.

Communications

By providing your email address or phone number, you consent to receiving campaign communications. You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email, replying STOP to any text message, or by contacting us directly at info@davidangelcc.com.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some portions of our website may not function properly.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics and form-processing providers. These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of your information. We encourage you to review them.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no Internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of information transmitted to our website.

Terms of Use

By using this website, you agree that the content is provided for informational and political purposes only. You may not reproduce, distribute, or use content from this website for commercial purposes without written permission from the Campaign.

We reserve the right to modify these policies at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

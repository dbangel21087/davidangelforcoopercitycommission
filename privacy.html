Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Name
  • Occupation
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Information submitted through contact forms or event RSVPs
  • Donation information submitted through our fundraising processor

B. Donations

Online contributions are processed by Anedot, a third-party payment processor. When you make a donation:

  • Your payment information is collected and processed by Anedot.
  • We receive information necessary to record your contribution (such as name, contact information, and contribution amount).

Anedot's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment information. We do not store credit card numbers on our website.

C. VAN (Voter Activation Network)

Information you submit through this website may be entered into our voter contact database (VAN) for campaign purposes, such as:

  • Voter outreach
  • Event communication
  • Volunteer coordination

Information placed into VAN is used solely for lawful campaign purposes.

D. Limited Automatic Technical Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider and fundraising processor may automatically log limited technical information such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Date and time of access

We do not use this information for advertising, tracking, or behavioral profiling. We do not use cookies, web beacons, advertising pixels, or similar tracking technologies. We do not run advertisements on this website.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Communicate with supporters
  • Send voting reminders and event information
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Process donations
  • Maintain internal campaign records
  • Comply with applicable election laws

We do not sell personal information. We do not use your information for commercial advertising.

3. Text Messaging

If you provide your phone number and consent to receive text messages, we may send:

  • Voting reminders
  • Event information

We do not send donation solicitations via text. We do not use automated dialing systems. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP.

4. Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary for campaign operations and compliance with applicable election laws.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date.
